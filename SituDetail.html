    Mobile Apps

    SITU Mobile is an Unpas integrated academic information system application, this application will be used by students and lecturers to access academic information such as: class schedules, grade lists, print credits. Previously students and lecturers used the SITU Website. My main task is to convert SITU Website design (Desktop Device) into SITU Mobile (Mobile device).

    Problem i want to solve.
    Students and lecturers at the UNPAS Campus who complained about the interface and the mobility of the UNPAS website service.

    My Role In the Project, Who Else in the project and My responsibility.
    My role as an UI/UX Designer, I work with Project Manager, Team Leader, Mobile app development team, Analytics team and Server and Database team. My responsibility is to build an experience and user interface for the SITU Mobile Apps that will be used by students and lecturers at UNPAS.

    A challenging and interesting problem for me.
    Converting experiences and interfaces that are already available on the website into the form of a mobile application. Change of exploration space from website layout to mobile device layout. also understand 2 different user characters, students and lecturers.

    The Solutions i gave.
    With the 2 problems above,from the results of my research i decided to use 5 Plane UX design method by Jesse James Garrett to build the user experience and choose a Modern Minimalist Design Style for the interface, so that students and lecturers can easily understand the product

    Why did I choose this solution?
    Because the method used to design the user experience is a derived method from UCD (User Centered Design), in other words the 5 Planes UX method is a concise version of UCD and is easy to implement into projects that have limited resources while still involving and focusing on the user. The modern minimalist design style was chosen because at this time, a lot of software uses this design style, so that prospective users from various circles will easily understand the interface design style.

    The impact of my final solution.
    the final solution that I provide has a good impact on 3 main aspects of this project: users, business, and developers. A survey was conducted by distributing questionnaires and demonstrating the application to more than 8 respondents from students and lecturers, the survey results obtained were satisfactory.

    In this project I learned how to design for 2 different generations, youth and parents which were represented by students and lecturers respectively. This is a challenge for me in designing. The lesson I learned on this project is to stick to design principles and ux laws as a guide in designing, because those 2 things work for an entire generation.
