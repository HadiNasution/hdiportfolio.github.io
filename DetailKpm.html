    Design mockups for PMB Yarsi Apps.

    Internship

    Feb 2021 - Apr 2022

    Mobile Apps

    PMB Yarsi is a new admissions mobile application, helps users to register as a student in Yarsi University. Inside there are 7 stages of registration to become a student, in which the user must fill out a form at each stage.

    Problem i want to solve.
    Yarsi is a private university domiciled in Jakarta, they have problems in the admission process which is still not flexible and relies too much on the old website. Therefore, it takes software that can run on mobile devices that can be a bridge between prospective new students and the Yarsi campus.

    My Role In the Project, Who Else in the project and My responsibility.
    My role as an UI/UX Designer, I work with Project Manager, Team Leader, Mobile app development team, Analytics team and Server and Database team. My responsibility is to build an experience and user interface for the PMB Yarsi Apps that will be used by an application that will be used by prospective students who want to join Yarsi University.

    A challenging and interesting problem for me.
    The main problem is designing the user experience and user interface in the project lies in the amount and flow of information that must be conveyed to the user, a considerable amount of information is in the form of a new student admission form which in filling out the form must follow several mandatory stages until the new student admission process done.

    The Solutions i gave.
    So that users do not get bored and have difficulty following the form filling stage in the limited scope of the smartphone screen. I decided to use a simple form wizard and design the form as minimally as possible, so that the user's focus is not diverted on useless elements.

    Why did I choose this solution?
    By using a form wizard in filling out the form, users don't have to worry about the stages in the new student admissions process. Users just have to fill out a simple form and let the app direct all the procedures.

    The impact of my final solution.
    I really look forward to getting some feedback on my work on this project. Unfortunately, this project has been postponed for an indefinite period due to internal factors that I certainly can't explain.

    The lesson I took for my second project was to turn complexity into simplicity. That's a very heavy thing. There are so many forms that the user has to fill out, I have to simplify it. I am responsible for users to easily and quickly complete the 7 stages of registration using a mobile application.
