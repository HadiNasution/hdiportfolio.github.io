Based on the development of the UX Study Case for a document management platform for ISO 27001 audits, it can be concluded that this platform can assist companies in preparing for ISO 27001 audits efficiently and effectively.

Features such as document management, access rights management, and document version tracking can help users to meet the requirements of the ISO 27001 standard.

The personas developed in this study, such as super admin, admin company, consultant, auditor, reviewer company, and editor company, represent diverse users and have different needs.

During the research phase, surveys, interviews and direct observation of users were carried out, as well as an analysis of the requirements of the ISO 27001 standard to understand user needs and the requirements that must be met by the document management platform.

In the design process, the Design Thinking method was used and prototyping was carried out to test the features developed. After testing, it was found that users experienced increased efficiency and satisfaction in using the platform.

In the testing phase, testing of the platform is carried out using the user testing method to ensure that this platform meets user needs and makes it easier for them to prepare for an ISO 27001 audit. In conclusion, a document management platform for ISO 27001 audits can help companies prepare for audits efficiently and effectively, and can provide a good and satisfying user experience.
