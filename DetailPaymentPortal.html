üß© Problem i want to solve.

Qwords received complaints about the first version of the payment portal, these complaints came from users and their internal team. Their users complained about the difficulty of the purchase process and configuring the product they wanted to buy. From their internal team, they complained about the payment portal, which looked outdated and was not friendly to ordinary users. I am responsible for solving the two problems above.
